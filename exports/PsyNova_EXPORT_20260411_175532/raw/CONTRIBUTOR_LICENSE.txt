CONTRIBUTOR AGREEMENT — CLINICAL OPERATIONS SYSTEM (COS)
Version 1.0 — Internal reference

1. DEFINITIONS
   "The Architect" means the sole author of the COS workbook generation logic,
   formulas, sheet structure, and Python build tooling in this repository.

   "The Partner" means the party operating the clinic under this model (data entry,
   roster, and day-to-day use of the workbook outputs).

2. OWNERSHIP OF LOGIC
   The Architect retains exclusive ownership of all logic, algorithms, sheet
   layouts, naming conventions, and source code used to generate and maintain
   the COS financial workbook. No transfer of intellectual property is granted
   by use of generated files alone.

3. ROLE OF THE PARTNER (OPERATOR)
   The Partner is granted a limited, revocable right to use generated workbooks
   and outputs for internal clinical and administrative operations. The Partner
   does not acquire rights to the generator source code except as separately
   licensed in writing.

4. RESTRICTIONS
   Redistribution of the generator or derivative build scripts, or removal of
   proprietary notices, requires prior written authorization from The Architect
   or the designated holding entity.

5. GOVERNING DOCUMENT
   For license terms on materials, see LICENSE.md. This file clarifies roles
   only and is not a substitute for a executed contract where one exists.
